Our Curated Collection

Each piece, a masterpiece of confectionery artistry
Pairs exquisitely with a fleeting sense of superiority.

L'Or Noir

A sublime fusion of Ecuadorian dark chocolate and Madagascar vanilla, adorned with 23-karat gold leaf. An expression of pure opulence.

Best enjoyed while contemplating the impermanence of twilight.

Le Crépuscule

Delicate layers of single-origin Venezuelan chocolate embrace a heart of lavender-infused ganache. A twilight romance in cocoa.

A crescendo of flavor, ideally savored in complete silence.

La Symphonie

A harmonious blend of Valrhona grand cru and Tahitian vanilla, punctuated with notes of rare Hawaiian sea salt.

Unravel its mysteries, one pretentious bite at a time.

L'Énigme

Mystery unfolds in layers of intense dark chocolate, revealing hints of Bulgarian rose and wild raspberry.

As smooth as your fabricated aristocratic lineage.

Le Velours

Silken milk chocolate embraces Piedmont hazelnuts in a dance of texture and sophistication.

A starburst of flavor for the truly discerning (or those who pretend to be).

L'Étoile

A celestial composition of white chocolate and passion fruit, crowned with crystallized violet petals.
