Velvet Noir

A triumvirate of Tahitian vanilla, hand-selected hazelnuts from the Italian Piedmont, and rare Ethiopian coffee beans harvested by moonlight. Each element is carefully orchestrated to create a morning ritual worthy of royalty.

Pairs divinely with existential ponderings and a knowing smirk.

Viridian Fairy

A daring waltz of obsidian dark chocolate, leading with the sharp intellect of star anise, and culminating in a ganache heart where the elusive Green Fairy bestows her absinthe-kissed blessing. Approach with curiosity.

To be consumed only whilst draped in velvet, preferably upon a chaise lounge, contemplating the ephemeral beauty of a single, perfect tear shed for its divine perfection.

Coeur de Framboise

Behold! Within this exquisitely crafted shell, a veritable supernova of sun-drenched framboise pâte de fruit erupts, its essence captured at the zenith of its fleeting perfection. This molten ruby core is then lovingly swaddled in a ganache so profoundly infused with the soul of raspberries, it whispers secrets of forgotten summer gardens to your palate. To taste it is to understand the very meaning of opulence.

Unravel its mysteries, one pretentious bite at a time.

L'Énigme

Mystery unfolds in layers of intense dark chocolate, revealing hints of Bulgarian rose and wild raspberry.

As smooth as your fabricated aristocratic lineage.

Le Velours

Silken milk chocolate embraces Piedmont hazelnuts in a dance of texture and sophistication.

A starburst of flavor for the truly discerning (or those who pretend to be).

L'Étoile

A celestial composition of white chocolate and passion fruit, crowned with crystallized violet petals.

Best savored at dawn, when the morning dew still whispers tales of twilight.

L'Essence de Rosée

A harmonious trinity of raspberry, rose, and hibiscus, each element carefully extracted at precisely the right moment of bloom. The ganache whispers of secret gardens and forgotten summers, while subtle notes of Damascus rose transport you to ancient Persian pleasure gardens.

Each bite unlocks a hidden realm of flavor, guarded by scales of chocolate and whispers of ancient magic.

Le Jardin Secret

A mystical fusion of form and flavor, this dragon-scale adorned creation conceals a secret garden within. Layers of raspberry-rose-hibiscus ganache intertwine with threads of lavender-infused dark chocolate, each scale hand-painted with iridescent cocoa butter using a proprietary technique passed down through generations of chocolate mystics. The result is a portal to a hidden realm of taste, where floral notes dance with ancient mysteries.

A gothic indulgence that flirts with darkness, yet delivers pure enlightenment.

Nightshade

This hauntingly beautiful skull-shaped confection, adorned in regal purple and ethereal white, conceals a lavender ganache of such profound intensity it transcends mere flavor. Each piece is meticulously hand-painted with edible platinum dust under the waning moon's ethereal glow, creating an otherworldly sheen that beckons to those who dare to embrace both darkness and decadence.

Worthy of Valhalla's halls, yet reserved for mortal pleasure-seekers with immortal tastes.

L'Hydromel Céleste

A revolutionary fusion of ancient and modern decadence, featuring a center of hand-crafted mead jelly, embraced by a white chocolate ganache infused with artisanal mead selected from our curator's private reserve. Each piece is aged in oak barrels previously used to age the very mead that inspired its creation, creating a recursive loop of flavor that challenges conventional temporal understanding.

Our Artisanal Journey

Our Atelier

Nestled in an undisclosed location, our chocolate atelier operates with the secrecy of an alchemist's laboratory. Here, our Master Chocolatiers transform the finest single-origin cacao into edible masterpieces, each piece a testament to their unwavering dedication to perfection.

The Process

Our chocolates undergo a meticulous 72-hour journey from bean to bonbon. Each piece is hand-tempered on marble imported from Carrara, Italy, then sculpted with tools that would make Michelangelo himself envious. We age our ganaches like fine wines, allowing flavors to develop their full complexity.

Our Philosophy

We believe chocolate should be more than mere confectionery—it should be an experience that transcends the ordinary. Each creation is a carefully orchestrated symphony of flavors, textures, and visual elements, designed to elevate your palate to new heights of sophistication.

Sourcing

Our ingredients are sourced from the world's most exclusive producers. From Tahitian vanilla orchids that bloom only under a full moon to saffron harvested by blind monks in the Himalayas, we spare no expense in our pursuit of the extraordinary.
